MeganBot — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MeganBot collects the following information to provide its services:

  • Phone numbers: The account owner's mobile number and contact numbers designated by the owner for messaging.
  • Message content: The text of reminders, notifications, and alerts sent through the service.
  • Delivery status: Message delivery confirmations and error reports from the carrier.

2. How We Use Information

  • To send automated reminders, scheduling notifications, and security alerts.
  • To coordinate scheduling and logistics with contacts at the owner's request.
  • To maintain message logs for the owner's personal reference.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose phone numbers or message content to any third parties. Message data is processed through Twilio's API for delivery and is subject to Twilio's Privacy Policy.

Mobile information and messaging consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

4. Data Retention

Message logs are retained on the owner's personal server for reference. Recipients may request deletion of their number from the system at any time by texting STOP or contacting the owner directly.

5. Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out of all messages at any time by replying with any of the following keywords: STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, OPTOUT, REVOKE.

After opting out, the recipient will receive a confirmation message and will not receive further messages unless they reply START to resubscribe.

6. Data Security

All message data is stored on the owner's self-hosted server protected by encryption at rest and in transit. API credentials are stored securely and access is restricted to the account owner.

7. Carrier-Level Controls

STOP, HELP, and START keywords are handled automatically at the carrier level. These cannot be overridden by the sender.
