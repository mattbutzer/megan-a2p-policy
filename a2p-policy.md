## Description

MeganBot is a personal assistant service operated by Matt Butzer as a sole proprietor. The Twilio phone number (+1 405-467-3588) is used to send automated reminders, scheduling notifications, and follow-up messages on behalf of the owner. Messages may be sent to the owner's phone or to contacts for scheduling, reservations, and coordination purposes.

## Use Cases

### SMS Messaging

- **Appointment reminders** — Scheduled reminders for meetings, appointments, and deadlines
- **Follow-up messages** — Coordination with contacts for reservations, plans, and scheduling
- **Daily briefings** — Summary of upcoming events, weather, and schedule for the day
- **Task notifications** — One-time reminders for time-sensitive tasks and follow-ups
- **Confirmation messages** — Booking confirmations, appointment verifications, and status updates

### Voice Calls

- **Appointment reminders** — Spoken delivery of time-sensitive reminders
- **Coordination calls** — Brief calls to confirm appointments or reservations

## Opt-In Flow

Recipients may opt in to receive messages from MeganBot by:
- Providing their phone number for scheduling or appointment coordination
- Confirming via SMS that they wish to receive reminders and follow-ups

## Message Frequency

- **Primary recipient:** Occasional reminders, briefings, and task notifications during active hours
- **Third-party contacts:** Infrequent — only for scheduling, reservations, and coordination as needed
- **Active hours:** 9:00 AM – 10:00 PM CDT
- **Silent hours:** Messages are not sent between 10:00 PM and 9:00 AM CDT

## STOP / HELP / Keywords

- **STOP** — Opt out of all SMS messages from this number
- **HELP** — Display help information with contact details
- **UNSTOP** — Resume receiving messages after STOP

These keywords are handled automatically by the carrier. Once STOP is received, no further messages will be sent to that number until UNSTOP is received.

## Data Privacy

- No message content is stored beyond 30 days
- No third-party data sharing
- No marketing or promotional content
- All messages are personal assistant communications (reminders, scheduling, coordination)
