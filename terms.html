MeganBot — Terms and Conditions

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Service Description

MeganBot is a personal assistant SMS and voice service operated by Matt Butzer as a sole proprietor. The service uses the phone number +1 (405) 467-3588 to send automated reminders, scheduling notifications, follow-up messages, and security alerts on behalf of the account owner.

This service is for personal use only. Messages are sent solely at the direction of the account owner and are not used for third-party marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

2. Message Types

  • Reminders and Notifications: Calendar reminders, appointment notifications, and scheduling follow-ups sent to the account owner or contacts designated by the owner.
  • Security Alerts: High-priority security monitoring alerts from the owner's self-hosted server monitoring system, sent to the owner's personal mobile phone.
  • Coordination Messages: Scheduling, reservation, and logistical coordination messages sent to contacts at the owner's request.

No messages are sent for third-party marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

3. Consent

Messages are sent only to phone numbers that the account owner has configured. Recipients can reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from all messages. Reply START to resubscribe. Standard message and data rates may apply.

4. Opt-Out Keywords

The following keywords will immediately unsubscribe the sender: STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, OPTOUT, REVOKE.

5. Help Keywords

Replying with HELP or INFO will return: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Msg and Data Rates May Apply."

6. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Reminders and notifications are sent as needed based on the owner's schedule. Security alerts are sent only when high-severity events are detected, typically no more than a few per week.

7. No Third-Party Sharing

Phone numbers and message content are not shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third parties. Message logs are retained only for the owner's personal reference. Mobile information and messaging consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
